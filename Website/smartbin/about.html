What We Do

Strong waste administration is a major test in urban cities for a large portion of the nations all through the world. There are numerous advances that are utilized for waste accumulation and for well managed recycling. The process of gathering the information is enormous and complex. Also the process of garbage collection is manual hence requiring a lot of effort and is time-consuming. In this project, we will reduce the complexities and the manual attention required; by using automated techniques for detecting the garbage level in the garbage can.
